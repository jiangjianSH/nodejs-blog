How to use quotation tag rightly in html page?

<q> for short quotation

For Example:

WWF's goal is to : Build a future where people live in harmony with nature.

<blockquote> for long quotation

For Example:

For 50 years, WWF has been protecting the future of nature. The world's leading conservation organization, WWF works in 100 countries and is supported by 1.2 million members in the United States and close to 5 million globally.

<add> use for abbreviation

WWW is a great technology

<cite> use to represent a reference to a creative work

If you want more about html, please refer to detail

<bdo> to override the current text direction

I am normal

I am normal

Use <addr> to represent author contact information
